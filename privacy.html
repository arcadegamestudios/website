Privacy Policy

v1.0 — last updated 7th Sep, 2025

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information of our players using our Android/iOS games (collectively, the “Games”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you play our Games.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information, depending on the specific Game:

  1. Device & Usage Information
    • Device type, operating system, language, and IP address.
    • Gameplay events (e.g., progress, scores, session length).
    • Crash reports, diagnostics, and performance data.

  2. Advertising & Analytics Data
    • Through Google AdMob, we may collect information necessary to serve ads (including your device identifiers, ad interactions, and whether you’ve chosen personalized or non-personalized ads).
    • Through GameAnalytics, we may collect anonymized gameplay data to improve our Games.

  3. Account Information (if applicable)
    • Some Games may allow or require you to sign in. In such cases, we may collect your email address, username, or other identifiers you provide.

  4. User-Generated Content (if applicable)
    • In Games that allow saving progress or uploading content to our servers, we may store your game data, preferences, and settings.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Games.
  • Personalize gameplay and ads (if you consent to personalized ads).
  • Analyze performance and usage trends.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical or security issues.
  • Communicate with you if sign-in or support services are available.

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Some Games store all data (such as progress, preferences, or settings) only on your device.
  • Server Storage: Some Games may store data (such as account details or gameplay progress) on our servers.

4. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time from within the Game by going to Settings > Discard Data to remove stored data from your device and/or our servers. You may remove any user data that may be stored by Google by going into your Google Account settings in accordance with their privacy policies.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Games may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying ads. See Google's Privacy Policy
  • GameAnalytics — for analyzing game usage. See GameAnalytics Privacy Policy
These third-party services have their own privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Games are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the age required by local law). If we discover that a child has provided personal information without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, do note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights such as:

  • Accessing your data.
  • Requesting deletion of your data.
  • Opting out of personalized ads.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within this page.
