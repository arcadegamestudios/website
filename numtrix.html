How to Play NumTrix

The core objective of NumTrix is simple: combine & match numbers, and achieve the highest score before time runs out. The speed and strategy are entirely up to you!

  • 1. Tap to Select: Tap on numbers in the grid to select them.
  • 2. Add & Match: Combine adjacent or related numbers to form matches.
  • 3. Beat the Clock: Every successful move gets you points, but the pressure is always on to make strategic choices quickly.
